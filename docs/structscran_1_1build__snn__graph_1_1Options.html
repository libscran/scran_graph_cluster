◆ num_neighbors

int scran::build_snn_graph::Options::num_neighbors = 10

The number of nearest neighbors to use for graph construction. Larger values increase the connectivity of the graph and reduce the granularity of subsequent community detection steps, at the cost of speed. Only relevant for the compute() overloads without pre-computed neighbors.

◆ weighting_scheme

Scheme scran::build_snn_graph::Options::weighting_scheme = Scheme::RANKED

Weighting scheme for each edge, based on the number of shared nearest neighbors of the two nodes.
